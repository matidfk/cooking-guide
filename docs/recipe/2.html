Soviet glazed cottage cheese bars

mmmmm

Ingredients:

  • 300g Cottage cheese
  • 40g Soft butter
  • 40g Sugar
  • 1pinch Salt
  • 2tsp Vanilla extract
  • 80g Milk chocolate
  • 20ml Vegetable oil

Instructions:

  1. Mix all the ingredients (Aside from chocolate and oil) until you get a creamy paste-like consistency. Add the mix to a piping bag and make small little bar shapes on a tray. Then place them into the freezer until they are set.

  2. While the bars are being frozen, you have plenty of time to make the glaze: melt chocolate on a bain-marie, add oil and stir with a spatula until very smooth and glossy. It should be quite hot and runny right before glazing.

  3. When the bars are completely set, take them out of the freezer. Working with one at a time, string a cold curd bar on a skewer or a toothpick and dip in hot glaze or just cover from all sides using a tablespoon. It needs to be done quite fast.

  4. Wrap in tinfoil and place back in the freezer to store them.
