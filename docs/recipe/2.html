South East asian fruit salad

Light zesty and tangy, a great pick me up for breakfast or a snack later in the day

Ingredients:

  • 1 Red chilli
  • 3tsp Tamarind paste
  • 1tsp Palm sugar
  • 1 Lime
  • 0 Cooked peanuts
  • 0 Pineapple
  • 0 Green apple
  • 0 Pear
  • 0 Cucumber
  • 0 Mango

Instructions:

  1. Roll the chilli in the palm of your hand to bring out its flavour. Next finely chop the chilli. Add some of the seeds, the more seeds you add the spicer it will be. Add the chopped chilli, tamarind paste and palm sugar to a bowl. Then add the zest and juice of the lime to the bowl (Zest is the grated skin).

  2. Either toasted or buy toasted peanuts. Crush the peanuts up so you get small little chunks and then add that to the bowl of ingredients and mix very well.

  3. Then on a plate, cut all the fruit into small to medium sized chunks. Make sure not to add the watery seedy centre of the cucumber as it will water down the sauce we made and make it too runny.

  4. Once all the fruit is chopped up add the sauce and mix them together well, make sure all the fruit is evenly coated and then itâ€™s ready to serve.
